This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process, use, share and protect information about you. It also tells you how you can access and update your information and make certain choices.

Scheme Context

For the implementation of Central Sector Scheme "Promotion of Agricultural Mechanization for In-situ management of crop residue" in the State of Punjab. There are 22 Districts in the State which are further Sub-Divided into 145 Blocks. The implementation of the scheme is supervised by the respective District Collectors and the Chief Agricultural Officers. The Chief Agriculture Officers are responsible for all the activities related to development and promotion of agriculture in the district.

Introduction

  • Burning of crop residue causes phenomenal pollution problems in the atmosphere and huge nutritional loss and physical health deterioration to the soil.
  • Financial assistance @50% of the cost and @80% of the cost on purchase of machinery/ equipment for management of crop residues respectively to individual farmer and cooperative societies/ panchayats/FPOs.

Objective

To create the online version of existing paper-based application of the scheme, which includes collection of applications from the intended beneficiaries, scrutiny/approval of the application, physical verification and disbursement of subsidy to the farmers.

Objectives of the App

  • To streamline the process of implementation of the scheme and make it more assessable and transparent.
  • To make the implementation effective and timely execution of the scheme.

Permissions Required

  • Camera: To capture images of machinery and documents for verification.
  • Gallery/Storage: To upload images/documents stored on your device.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, mobile number, Aadhaar number, and address/location.
  • Device Information: Device ID, IP address, OS version.
  • Uploaded Content: Images or videos for verification.

Use of Information

  • For verification and validation of subsidy claims.
  • To process applications and transfer benefits.
  • To maintain accountability and transparency of scheme implementation.

Security

Your data is stored securely and only accessible to authorized personnel as per government policy.
