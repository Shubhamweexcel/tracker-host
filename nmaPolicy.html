Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 06, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how NMA Punjab (Nasha Mukti Abhiyan) (the “App”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information. The App is officially operated and managed by the Department / Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of Punjab, India as part of a public health program for substance abuse prevention, awareness, and rehabilitation.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the App and fulfill official program objectives.

1.1 Information You Provide

  • User identification details: Name, official designation, phone number, and email address (where applicable).
  • Program documentation: Meeting reports, attendance records, photographs, videos, and documents uploaded for official reporting.
  • Communications: Feedback, messages, and notes submitted through in-app forms.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • App usage data and diagnostic logs
  • Crash reports and performance metrics

This information is used strictly for system stability, security, and performance improvement.

2. App Permissions

Camera & Media Access
  • Camera: Used only to capture official program-related images or documents.
  • Photo Library / Storage: Used only to upload existing official documents or images.

Permissions are requested only when required. You may deny or revoke permissions at any time via device settings; however, certain features may not function without them.

3. How We Use Information

  • To document and monitor program activities
  • To generate internal reports for authorized government officials
  • To ensure operational communication related to the program
  • To maintain application security, integrity, and performance

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Data processing is carried out in accordance with applicable Indian laws and government policies. Processing is based on public interest, official authority, and where applicable, user consent.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

Information may be shared only with:

  • Authorized officers of the Government of Punjab involved in the program
  • Government-approved service providers for hosting, analytics, or technical support
  • Lawful authorities where disclosure is required by law

We do not sell, rent, or use personal data for advertising or commercial purposes.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only for the duration necessary to fulfill official program requirements, auditing obligations, and legal compliance.

7. Data Security

We apply reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards including secure servers, access controls, and encrypted data transmission. Despite these measures, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

8. User Rights

  • Access and review personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion where permitted by law
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

9. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended solely for authorized adult users. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18 years of age.

10. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on third-party infrastructure for hosting and diagnostics. Such providers operate under strict contractual and security obligations.

11. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect legal or operational changes. Updates will be published within the App.
