NMA Punjab – App Support

Official support page for the Nasha Mukti Abhiyan mobile application operated by the Department of Health & Family Welfare, Government of Punjab.

About the App

NMA Punjab (Nasha Mukti Abhiyan) is an official mobile application used by authorized program staff to record program activities, meetings, and feedback as part of the state public health initiative.

Account Deletion & Data Removal

Users may request deletion of their account and associated personal data by sending an email to the official support address with the subject line “Account Deletion Request – NMA Punjab App”.

Requests are processed within a reasonable timeframe in accordance with applicable laws and program policies.
