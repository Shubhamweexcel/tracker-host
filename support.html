NMA Punjab – Support

Official support and privacy information for the Nasha Mukti Abhiyan Mobile Application, Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Punjab.

About the NMA Punjab App

NMA Punjab (Nasha Mukti Abhiyan) is a mobile application developed by the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Punjab, for authorized staff to capture Reference IDs, feedback, and meeting details to support substance-abuse prevention and rehabilitation efforts across the state.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how NMA Punjab (Nasha Mukti Abhiyan) collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Profile & contact details: Name, phone number, email.
  • Meeting documents & media: Photos, videos, and documents you upload.
  • Messages & feedback: Notes and feedback you submit.

1.2 Device & usage data

Device model, OS version, app version, crash logs, and usage statistics.

2. Permissions: Camera & Gallery

Camera and gallery permissions are used only for uploading meeting-related documents and media.

  • Camera: Capture photos of documents or materials.
  • Gallery/Storage: Upload existing media files.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Manage and store uploaded documents.
  • Generate reports for authorized staff.
  • Send program-related updates.
  • Improve app performance and security.

4. Legal Basis & Public Interest

Data is processed for public health purposes under the Nasha Mukti Abhiyan.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • Authorized government officers.
  • Secure third-party service providers.
  • When required by law.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained as required for program goals, compliance, and audit purposes.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards including HTTPS and secure cloud hosting.

8. Your Rights

Users may request access, correction, deletion, or withdrawal of consent by contacting support.

9. Children

The App is intended for adult staff only and not for children under 18.

10. Third-Party Services

Hosting, analytics, and crash reporting providers may process data on our behalf.

11. International Transfers

Transfers outside India are safeguarded as per applicable laws.

12. Changes to This Policy

Policy updates will be posted within the App with a new effective date.

13. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact any zone or the Ministry using the contact details provided above.
