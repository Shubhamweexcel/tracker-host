NMA Punjab – App Support

Official support page for the Nasha Mukti Abhiyan mobile application operated by the Department of Health & Family Welfare, Government of Punjab.

About the App

NMA Punjab (Nasha Mukti Abhiyan) is an official mobile application used by authorized program staff to capture program activity details, meeting records, and feedback in support of substance abuse prevention and rehabilitation efforts across Punjab.
